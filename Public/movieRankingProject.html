Best Movies According to Spirit

These are my top 3 movies of all-time.

Labyrinth - 1986

Labyrinth is by far my number 1 movie of all-time. It has fantastical elements and is a great coming of age story. I have seen it probably a few dozen times and can likely recite it line after line.

Legend - 1985

Legend is one of my favorite movies. Tim Curry plays a magnificant and tempting demon and Tom Cruise is the hero. Long before my birth but this one is another childhood classic for me.

Firefly/Serenity - 2002/2005

Sadly this TV series and accompanying movie never got to take off but is a favorite of many a nerd. Space. Opera. It is funny. Take a watch!
